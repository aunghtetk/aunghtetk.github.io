About Kyaw Floral's Flower Shop

Established in 1966 by Kyaw floral, Kyaw floral's Flower Shop continues to be family owned and operated florist. Kyaw floral's Flower Shop a proud family tradition where we excel at customer service, creativity & artistry in the floral market.

We would love for you to see our passion displayed in our 1500 sq. ft. showroom at 487 Laurier Ave. Abundant parking, fresh local & imported flowers daily, unique plants/succulents & exquisite gifts. We offer beautiful hand-tied bouquets, Modern, European arrangements and a wide variety of plants and orchids. Plentiful inventory & experienced designers on hand for those who favor a personal selection of floral delights. Voted" #1 Diamond Florist" for many years. We are proud of our integrity & committed to providing you professional service that will nurture our long-term relationship with you. We are extremely proud to be a third-generation family business serving Milton & area since 1966. We invite you to browse our website & hope you have the opportunity to visit our beautiful store.

Over the 55 years of business, with learning the floral industry, we are still to this day improving ourselves, each and every day to make your floral sending experience fantastic.

To service the ever-growing client base, we are always upgrading our computer software and product offering. The www.karensflowershop.ca website is modified, updated and improved constantly. To have a wide variety of product selection and new floral trends. Karen's Flower Shop is extremely proud to be an award-winning florist in the Greater Toronto Area.

With a fleet of florist delivery vehicles that cover a large area of the GTA and surrounding areas, which allows us to offer unsurpassed customer service.
